Holiday Happiness

Here are some recommandation for holiday movies that are not only funny but heartwarming as well. To remind all of us that during this busy season to value your time with your love ones and that its not really about the gifts.

How Grinch Stole Christmas

"Maybe Christmas, he thought, doesn't come from a store. Maybe Christmas...perhaps means a little bit more!"

Whatever holiday that you celebrate, celebrating with your love ones what makes it special.

A Charlie Brown Christmas

"It's not what's under the Christmas tree that matters. It's who around it."

Nothing is more valuable than the time you spend with the people you cherish.

Elf

"Santa's coming! I know him! I know him!"

You are never too old or too young to be excited for anything.

Little present from me to you

As you choose a film to watch, it is time to make a hot cup of hot chocolate to enjoy with your love ones.

This recipe is created by Binging with Babish and is inspired by The Simpsons Movie .

  • Whole Milk

  • 1/2 Cup of Ghirardelli Cocoa Powder

  • 1/2 Cup of Sugar
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Small Pinch of Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 Tsp of Instant Coffee
  • Whipped Cream
  • Chocolate Shavings
  • 1 Small Graham Cracker
  • 1 Marshmallow
